Dr Reddys Foundation

Dr. Reddy's Foundation (DRF) is a not-for-profit organization, focused on enhancing the dignity and well-being of low-income households.

About

We believe that learning should be an active and engaging process that challenges students to think critically, solve problems, and collaborate with others. Our teachers are committed to creating a learning environment that fosters curiosity, creativity, and a love of learning. Through personalized instruction, hands-on experiences, and meaningful feedback, we strive to empower our students to reach their full potential and become lifelong learners. Whether in the classroom or beyond, we aim to instill in our students a passion for learning and a desire to make a positive impact on the world.
